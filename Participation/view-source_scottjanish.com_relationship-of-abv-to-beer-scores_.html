<h1 class="entry-title">Relationship of ABV to Beer Scores</h1>

</div> <!-- .et_post_meta_wrapper -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Probably not a shocker, but interesting to see the data, it appears the average ABV across a given beer category <span style="text-decoration: underline;">does</span> increase the average beer score for that particular category. A few noticeable outliers are goses and berliner weisses which tend to score high despite their low ABV, and American malt liquors score low despite their relatively high alcohol content.</p>
<p>The chart below shows Beeradvocate’s average rAVG score for each beer category (essentially the average of all individual beer averages for each beer category) in a scatter plot with the beer category average ABV.</p>
